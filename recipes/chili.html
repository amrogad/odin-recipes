Chili

Description

This hearty chili is a comforting blend of lean ground beef, rich tomato sauce, and a mix of kidney and chili beans that add texture and flavor. Seasoned with just the right amount of chili powder and salt, it delivers a satisfying warmth that's perfect for chilly evenings. Whether served alone or with your favorite toppings, this dish is sure to please with its robust taste and simplicity.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 15 ounces tomato sauce
  • 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
  • 1 (15 ounce) can chili beans, not drained
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder, or to taste
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
